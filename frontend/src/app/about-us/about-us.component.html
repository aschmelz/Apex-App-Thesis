Apex Wholesale Supply Co.

At Apex Wholesale Supplies, we're more than just a wholesale company; we're a family. Our story began 50 years ago when we decided to pursue our passion for supplying families with the best home products, driven by a passion for home developement and a desire to build something lasting, founded this company. With a commitment to quality and service, our great-grandfather laid the foundation for what has become a trusted name in the wholesale industry. Today, the legacy continues with his grandchildren who have taken the reins and continue to uphold the values that have made Apex a success. We remain a family-owned and operated business, where every employee is treated like part of the extended family.
